Oreo Truffles

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg Oreo cookies
  • 1 (8 oz) pkg cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (12 oz) pkg semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 3 Tbsp vegetable shortening

Steps

  1. Process Oreos to crumbs in a food processor
  2. Add cream cheese
  3. Continue to process until completely incorporated (this part could also be done in a mixer)
  4. Roll mixture into small balls
  5. Place on wax paper-lined baking sheet (you can place toothpicks in to make dipping later easier)
  6. Freeze until solid (at least 1 hour or up to days ahead)
  7. Microwave chocolate chips and shortening on 50% power until melted (about 4 minutes) stirring in between
  8. Use a tooth pick to dip balls in chocolate to coat all sides
  9. Return to baking sheet
  10. When set place in freezer for 15 minutes
  11. Take balls off baking sheet and place in container
  12. Store in fridge or freezer
