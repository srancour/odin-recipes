Lattice Top Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 can (10 3/4 oz) condensed cream of potato soup
  • 1 bag (16oz) frozen broccoli, carrots, and cauliflower vegetable combination, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup (4 oz) shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon Durkee Seasoned Salt
  • 1 can (2.8 oz) Durkee French Fried Onions
  • 2 cups (10 oz) cubed cooked chicken
  • 1 package (4 oz) refrigerator crescent rolls

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375ºF
  2. Combine soup, milk, seasoned salt, chicken, vegetables, 1/2 cup cheese, and 1/2 can French Fried Onions
  3. Place in 8x12-inch baking dish
  4. Bake covered at 375ºF for 20 minutes
  5. Unwrap crescent rolls and separated into two rectangles
  6. Press together perforated cuts
  7. Cut each rectangle lengthwise into 3 strips
  8. Place strips on casserole to from lattice top
  9. Bake uncovered 15 minutes longer
  10. Top lattice with remaining cheese and onions
  11. Bake uncovered 3-5 minutes or until onions are golden brown
