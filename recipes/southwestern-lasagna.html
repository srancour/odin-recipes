Southwestern Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 pkg dry taco mix
  • 4 soft taco shells (cut in half)
  • 24 oz salsa
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 8 oz shredded monterey jack or mexican style cheese
  • Corn taco shells (or tortilla chips), crushed

Steps

  1. Prepare meat as on taco mix package
  2. Set meat aside
  3. Pre-heat oven at 350ºF
  4. Grease 9"x9" pan
  5. Layer ingredients in order:
    1. 4 halves of soft taco shells, spread over bottom
    2. Half of the salsa
    3. Half of the sour cream
    4. Half of the meat mixture
    5. Half of the cheese
    6. Top with crushed taco shells
  6. Repeat layering for second layer
  7. Sprinkle salsa on top
  8. Bake at 350ºF for 30 minutes
